# Community Guardian AI — Demo Script

## Opening

Community Guardian AI is a decision intelligence platform designed to help communities and city stakeholders convert fragmented data into faster, more transparent, and human-reviewed decisions.

The platform combines BigQuery analytics, Vertex AI Gemini, predictive risk forecasting, multimodal citizen reporting, and workflow automation.

## Dashboard

This dashboard provides a high-level view of community conditions, including operational reports, unresolved issues, priority indicators, and zone-level signals.

Instead of requiring stakeholders to inspect separate systems manually, Community Guardian AI brings the most important signals into one workspace.

## Forecast Intelligence

This page identifies zones with elevated risk based on available community data.

The system detects trends and anomalies, then creates alerts for areas that may require stakeholder attention.

The forecast is decision support. It does not automatically deploy resources or make decisions without human review.

## AI Workflow Automation

When an anomaly alert is selected, Gemini generates an action-plan draft.

The plan includes practical operational steps, a recommended owner team, a priority level, and a response window.

A stakeholder reviews the recommendation and can approve or reject it.

After the decision, the platform stores an audit record in BigQuery. This creates transparency and accountability.

## AI Decision Center

Stakeholders can ask natural-language questions about the community data.

For example, they can ask which zone needs urgent attention, what issue type is increasing, or which action should be prioritized.

Gemini provides an evidence-based response using the available data and includes responsible-AI boundaries.

## Citizen Report

This page allows citizens or field teams to submit reports with text and image evidence.

Gemini can analyze the image to help classify the report, but the system does not make autonomous enforcement or public-service decisions.

## Closing

Community Guardian AI demonstrates how Google Cloud technologies can support safer, healthier, more efficient, and more sustainable communities.

The key principle is human-centered decision intelligence: AI helps stakeholders understand data and act faster, while people remain responsible for final decisions.
